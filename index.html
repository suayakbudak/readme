On the analyst side: requirements gathering, technical specifications, and coordination between development and stakeholders.

Not ideal timing — but early exposure to unpredictability builds adaptability.

PART 02 — The Five-Month Lesson

In September 2022, I joined a Big Four professional services firm as an IT Risk & Governance Consultant.

My focus: software asset management, IT governance frameworks, and risk assessments — including engagements with banking clients.

I built and maintained deliverables that were used in client engagements: asset inventories, compliance dashboards, and risk assessment reports. Tools and documentation that stakeholders relied on.

Client-facing work. Tight deadlines. High regulatory scrutiny in the banking sector.
